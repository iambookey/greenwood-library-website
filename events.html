Welcome to Greenwood Community Library

We are a public library serving the Greenwood community. Our mission is to provide access to information, education, and entertainment for people of all ages.

Features and Services

  • Over 50,000 books, audiobooks, and e-books
  • Free Wi-Fi and computer access
  • Programs for children, teens, and adults
  • Study rooms and meeting spaces
  • Interlibrary loan services
